100-word bio

Dr. Maryna Kuzmenko is co-founder and CEO of Petiole, an agritech company building practical AI tools for plant science, crop measurement, and agricultural decision support. Her work focuses on applying computer vision and mobile-first technologies to real agricultural problems, including leaf area measurement, plant health assessment, seedling quality, and visual phenotyping. Maryna is also an educator, speaker, and creator of AI in agriculture content, helping researchers, growers, entrepreneurs, and policymakers understand how artificial intelligence can be used responsibly and effectively across the food and farming sector.
