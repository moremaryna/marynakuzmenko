    <h2>Professional bio</h2>

    <div class="bio-card featured">
      <p>
        Dr. Maryna Kuzmenko brings nearly 10 years of practical experience in applying digital technologies in agriculture, with a primary focus on computer vision and deep learning for plant phenotyping and food quality assurance.
      </p>

      <p>
        She is co-founder and CEO of Petiole, a UK-based SME developing practical AI tools for agriculture and plant science. The global platform Petiole Pro, developed by Maryna’s team, was named a finalist in the GIST Tech-I 2015 competition in the USA, shortlisted for the UK FPC Fresh Awards 2023, and received two grants from Innovate UK — the UK government’s innovation agency — for practical AI applications in agricultural challenges such as pest and disease detection.
      </p>

      <p>
        Maryna was recognised as an <strong>Expert Top Voice on LinkedIn</strong> in the May 2026 edition and she shares her knowledge on other <strong>social media</strong> platforms.
      </p>

      <p>
        She is one of 16 Community Forum Members of 2025/26 Cohort, pre-selected by the Innovate UK Women in Innovation team. Maryna is also a fellow at Inspire Programme of the Oxford Farming Conference 2026.
      </p>
      <p>
      Maryna is a fellow of programme “Scaling young women’s businesses through IP mentorship” (World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO) + International Trade Centre’s (ITC) SheTrades initiative).
      </p>
      <p>
        She is a regular speaker at educational <a href="\events\">events</events> and has participated in high-level agriculture-focused meetings in the United Kingdom and internationally.
